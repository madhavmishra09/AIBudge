# Metrics

## North Star Metric
**Number of Completed AI Spend Audits per Month**  
- Why: Directly measures product adoption and value delivered.  
- Goal: 1,000+ audits/month within 6 months of launch.  

---

## Input Metrics

### Acquisition
- **Unique Visitors to Landing Page**  
- **Click‑through Rate (CTR) on “Run Your Free Audit” CTA**

### Activation
- **Audit Completion Rate** (audits started vs. finished)  
- **Average Time to First Result** (speed of audit engine)

### Engagement
- **Shareable Report Links Generated**  
- **Email Captures for Saved Reports**

### Retention
- **Repeat Audits per User** (founders/managers re‑auditing after changes)  
- **Return Rate within 30 Days**

### Monetization
- **Discounted Credit Purchases via Credex**  
- **Average Savings Identified per Audit**

---

## Pivot Triggers
- **Audit Completion Rate < 40%** → UX redesign needed.  
- **Shareable Links < 20% of audits** → Improve social proof & sharing incentives.  
- **Savings Identified < $100 median** → Re‑evaluate pricing data sources or audit logic.
